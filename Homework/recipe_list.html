Nutella Sandwich

Recipe By: Ivan

This delicious dish is a classic Nutella sandwich that is extremely easy to make and really tasty

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Nutella


Directions:

  • First, get two pieces of bread and some Nutella.
  • Next, get a knife and smudge the Nutella on the pieces bread.
  • Finally, put the pieces of bread together and your sandwich is ready to eat!
